Our service

COMPREHENSIVE MEP SERVICES

Integrated Mechanical, Electrical & Plumbing Solutions

At Golden Path, we provide comprehensive Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing (MEP) services covering the entire project lifecycle from design and engineering to installation, execution, and maintenance services. We ensure effective, reliable, and sustainable systems that meet our clients' needs and support their project continuity.

Request Consultation
01
Design & Engineering

Comprehensive integrated engineering design for mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems. Design of heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) systems. Design of electrical and plumbing networks with full technical specifications and compliance with international standards.

02
Installation & Implementation

Professional implementation of all MEP systems with the highest quality standards. Integration of air conditioning, electrical, and plumbing systems at project sites. Technical supervision and field support during implementation with continuous quality control.

03
CAD & BIM Modeling Services

Preparation of detailed drawings and 3D models for all MEP systems. Technical drawing and documentation works using Computer-Aided Design (CAD) software. Building Information Modeling (BIM) systems modeling with comprehensive clash detection and resolution throughout the design phase.

04
Analysis & Simulation

Comprehensive performance and energy efficiency analysis for all MEP systems. Hydraulic flow and pressure simulation for plumbing and HVAC systems. Electrical load calculations and detailed system analysis to ensure optimal performance and compliance with project requirements.

05
Maintenance & Services

Preventive and corrective maintenance programs for all MEP systems. Periodic services to ensure optimal performance and extend system lifespan. Rapid response emergency repair services with 24/7 availability for critical system failures.

06
Project Management

Complete planning, scheduling, and coordination of MEP projects. Quality control monitoring and ensuring compliance with all relevant standards and specifications. Supervision of compliance with safety requirements and regulations throughout project execution.

07
Testing & Commissioning

Initial commissioning of all MEP systems with comprehensive functionality checks. Performance testing and efficiency verification to ensure systems meet design specifications. Final handover with complete documentation including operation manuals, maintenance guides, and as-built drawings.

08
Energy Efficiency & Sustainability

Specialized consulting and solutions in energy efficiency for MEP systems. Design, implementation, and operation of sustainable systems with reduced environmental impact. Advisory support for global LEED certifications and other sustainability recognition programs.
