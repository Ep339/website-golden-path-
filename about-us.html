MEP Services FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What MEP services does Golden Path provide?

We offer comprehensive Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing services including design, installation, maintenance, BIM modeling, energy efficiency solutions, and project management for all types of construction projects.

Do you provide both design and installation services?

Yes, we offer complete turnkey solutions including MEP design, engineering, installation, testing, commissioning, and ongoing maintenance services.

What types of projects do you handle?

We serve residential, commercial, industrial, and institutional projects including offices, hospitals, hotels, shopping malls, factories, and residential complexes.

Do you provide BIM modeling services?

Yes, we offer advanced BIM modeling for MEP coordination, clash detection, 3D visualization, and construction documentation to ensure seamless integration of all systems.

What are your response times for emergency repairs?

We provide 24/7 emergency support with response times within 2-4 hours for critical issues, depending on your location and service agreement.

Do you offer energy efficiency solutions?

Yes, we provide energy audits, efficiency upgrades, sustainable design solutions, and can help you achieve LEED and other green building certifications.

What certifications do your technicians hold?

Our team includes certified engineers, licensed electricians, HVAC technicians, and plumbers with certifications from recognized international and local authorities.
